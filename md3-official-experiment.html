Checkbox & Switch rendered verbatim from @material/web (Material Design 3 web components). No Embassy tokens or branding applied.

Evaluation harness. These are the real official Material components loaded as ES-module custom elements (Lit + Shadow DOM) from the jsDelivr ESM CDN. Colors, spacing, ripples, focus rings, and motion are Material's defaults — the component internals live in Shadow DOM and are not styled by this page. Segmented Button and Snackbar are intentionally absent: material-web never shipped them.
